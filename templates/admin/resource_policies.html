Policy Structure

Base Requirement: All users must have paid:true in their attributes to access any resource.

Additional Conditions: Admins can set department, role, or other attribute requirements that are ANDed with the payment requirement.

Example: If you set a policy to dept:computerscience or role:faculty, the effective policy becomes paid:true and (dept:computerscience or role:faculty)
